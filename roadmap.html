30-Day Protocol

Your Transformation Roadmap

A day-by-day, phase-by-phase action plan. Follow the sequence. Trust the process. Everything changes.

1
Phase 1 · Days 1–7

DETOX

The hardest week. You are breaking patterns your brain has automated over months or years. Expect discomfort. That discomfort is the detox working.

🎯 Remove Triggers

Day 1

Trigger Audit

Write down every situation that leads you to compulsive behavior. Specific times, places, emotional states, and people. No judgment — just forensic honesty.

Day 2

App Purge

Delete or hide the top 3 most time-consuming apps. Move your phone to a different room during meals and the first hour after waking. Friction is your friend.

Day 3

Environment Redesign

Phone charger moves out of the bedroom. Replace it with a physical book and journal. Set up a physical workspace that is device-intentional, not device-adjacent.

Day 4

Digital Curfew Begins

All screens off 90 minutes before bed. Hard rule. Non-negotiable. If sleep is disrupted, that's withdrawal. Expected. Welcome it.

🔒 Digital Control

Day 5

Notification Detox

Turn off ALL non-essential notifications. Only calls from a list of 5 specific people may interrupt you. Everything else checks manually, twice per day maximum.

Day 6

Social Media Audit

Audit who you follow. Unfollow any account that does not educate, inspire, or genuinely connect you. Reduce your feed by 60%. Make it a tool, not a slot machine.

Day 7

24-Hour Digital Fast

One full day without social media, news, YouTube, or non-essential digital content. No cheating. This is the hardest day and the most important. You will survive it.

Week 1 Check-In

Reflection Day

Journal: What was hardest? What surprised you? What urges arose and how did you handle them? Review your trigger audit. Identify what worked and what didn't. Prepare for Phase 2.

2
Phase 2 · Days 8–21

CONTROL

You've done the detox. Now you build the systems that make sobriety automatic — and you start replacing old patterns with powerful new ones.

🌊 Urge Management

Days 8–9

Urge Surfing Practice

Begin the urge surfing technique (see Free System, Ch. 4). Every time an urge arises, use it as practice. Rate the urge. Breathe. Observe. Don't act. Log in your journal afterward.

Days 10–11

The 10-Minute Rule

Any time a compulsive urge appears, set a timer for 10 minutes before acting on it. Do something physical immediately. By the time the timer ends, the urge will have diminished by 50–80%.

Days 12–13

Implementation Intentions

Write 10 "If...Then" plans for your most common triggers. Practice reviewing them each morning. These scripts reprogram your automatic behavior in the moment of highest vulnerability.

Day 14

Week 2 Review

How many urges arose? How many did you navigate successfully? What patterns are emerging? Which triggers remain most challenging? Adjust your if-then plans accordingly.

🔄 Habit Replacement Stack

Days 15–16

Launch Your Exercise Habit

Begin the HUNTAI workout protocol. Week 2–3 level: 30 minutes, 4x per week. Two strength sessions. Two cardio. Schedule them in your calendar as immovable appointments.

Days 17–18

Deep Reading Practice

Read for 30 minutes daily — physical book only. Choose something that genuinely interests you. Your ability to follow a narrative thread is your attention span in training.

Days 19–20

Social Reconnection

Have one meaningful conversation (phone call or in-person) per day. No texts. Voice or face-to-face. This is the real social connection your brain craves — not broadcast scrolling.

Day 21

Three-Week Milestone

The average habit takes 21 days to begin solidifying. Celebrate with a genuine reward: a good meal, time in nature, something creative. Reflect on how different you feel compared to Day 1.

3
Phase 3 · Days 22–30

REBUILD

The most meaningful phase. Systems are in place. Habits are forming. Now you build the life you were meant to live — with focus, discipline, and intentional growth.

🎯 Deep Focus Protocols

Days 22–23

90-Minute Deep Work Blocks

Introduce structured deep work sessions. Phone on do-not-disturb in another room. One task. Timer set. No multitasking. Begin with 60 minutes and extend to 90 as your focus builds.

Days 24–25

Meditation Introduction

10 minutes daily. Use guided meditation if needed (Waking Up, Insight Timer apps — but time-boxed, not scrolled). Strengthen your metacognitive ability: observe your thoughts rather than being them.

Days 26–27

Discipline Architecture

Review your full daily routine. What is working? What needs adjustment? Create a "non-negotiable" list — 5 daily behaviors that define who you are becoming. Make them unnegotiable.

Days 28–29

Goal Setting & Vision Work

With a clear mind for the first time in months, set 3 real goals for the next 90 days. One health, one work/skill, one relationship. Write the specific actions required for each. Begin Day 1 of each.

🏆 Day 30: The Identity Declaration

🎯

Day 30 — You Made It

This is not the end. This is the foundation.

Write Your Identity Statement

"I am a person who [controls their attention / pursues meaningful work / shows up every day / ...]. I am not defined by my past habits. I am defined by my daily choices. I am in the process of becoming my best version."

Celebrate With Integrity

Mark Day 30 with something meaningful and earned. Not with a binge of the behavior you've overcome — but with something that reflects the person you've become. The celebration is a vote for your new identity.

What Comes Next

Day 31 is Day 1 of your actual life. The systems are in place. The habits are forming. The identity is shifting. Continue the daily routine. Add complexity. Raise the bar gradually. Never return to autopilot.

Share Your Story

You know someone who needs this. Share HUNTAI with them — anonymously, directly, however you can. Your transformation has the power to start someone else's.
